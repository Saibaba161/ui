Description: An inverted metallurgical microscope is a specialized optical instrument designed for examining opaque materials, typically metallic samples, in various fields such as metallurgy, material science, and quality control. Unlike traditional microscopes, the inverted design features the light source and condenser located below the specimen, while the objectives and eyepieces are positioned above.
