Radial Drilling Machine

  • Location: R.V.R & J.C College of Engineering
  • Base price: 500/-

Specifications:

  • Horizontal travel: 270 mm
  • Vertical travel: 525 mm
  • Work area: 770x530 mm
  • Max. drill size: 25 mm
  • Max. spindle travel: 100 mm

Description: A radial drilling machine consists of a large, column-mounted drill head with a radial arm that can be moved around the column. The drill head can be adjusted vertically on the arm, and the arm itself can rotate around the column horizontally. The machine typically has a worktable where the material to be drilled is secured.
