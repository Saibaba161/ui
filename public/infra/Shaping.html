Shaping Machine

Specifications:

  • Max stroke: 630 mm
  • Table traverse: 650x370 mm
  • No. of speeds: 4/10-80 Strokes/min
  • No. of Feeds: 5/0, 14-0.7 mm/stroke

Description: A shaping machine typically consists of a horizontal ram, on which a cutting tool is mounted, and a worktable where the workpiece is securely clamped. The ram moves back and forth in a linear motion, allowing the cutting tool to remove material from the workpiece during each stroke. The machine may have adjustable mechanisms for controlling the depth and length of the cut, allowing for precise shaping.
