Slotting Machine

Specifications:

  • Maximum length of stroke of slotter ram (mm): 400mm
  • Diameter of rotary table (mm): 915mm
  • Longitudinal movement of the table (mm): 762 mm
  • Cross Movement of the table (mm): 559 mm
  • Motor power (H.P): 7.5 HP
  • Number of speeds: 9
  • Floor space required: 3050 mm x 1800mm

Description: A slotting machine consists of a vertical ram, which houses the cutting tool, and a worktable where the workpiece is secured. The ram moves up and down in a vertical direction, allowing the slotted tool to cut into the material. The machine may have adjustments for controlling the depth and length of the slots. Some slotting machines are also equipped with a rotary table for angular cutting.
